Kazuki Tojo is an AeroAstro S.M.–Ph.D. candidate in MIT's Gas Turbine Lab. His interests lie in the fluid mechanics and heat transfer of propulsion and aerodynamics. He has completed a B.S.E. thesis on electric propulsion at Princeton University and an M.Eng. thesis on heat transfer at University of Oxford. His current research at MIT focuses on rotating stall, under the guidance of Professor Ed Greitzer.
